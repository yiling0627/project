Our Restaurant

        Head Chef Michael Bornmann who is working together with Sous-Chef Juliane Santini and they both offer you perfect Italian cooking, best served dishes with fresh ingredients and old recipes. We have only carefully sourced and seasonal ingredients in our disposal to make rustic dishes. Chefs have an experience of working in some of the finest kitchens in Berlin, New York and Paris. In collaboration with working background the kitchen creates modern, seasonal and focused on your pleasure dishes.

        The atmosphere of real Italy rules the mood at our restaurant. We offer you not only the best recipes of typical Italian dishes also the mood of many things: interior, music, waitresses and just Italian air! We put our hearts and souls into every dish we cook to help you feel the vibes of Italy and to transport you to the picturesque scenes of ancient times, outstanding aura and mood.
